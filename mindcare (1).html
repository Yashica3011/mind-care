Mental Reset

🌿 3-Minute Stress Relief Guide

Follow the steps below and use the timer. Even 3 minutes can reset your mind.

3:00

Press Start to begin your reset

1️⃣

Deep Breathing

Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 4. Repeat 3 times.

2️⃣

Body Scan

Gently relax your shoulders, jaw, and hands. Release tension you're holding.

3️⃣

Gentle Stretch

Roll your neck slowly, stretch your arms overhead, take a big yawn.

4️⃣

Mindful Moment

Name 3 things you can see, 2 you can hear, 1 you're grateful for.
